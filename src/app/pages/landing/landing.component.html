Why we built this

We got tired of
being the one who remembers.

Barabar was built because someone in a shared flat got tired of being the person who always remembered. Tired of the awkward reminder texts. Tired of the mental tab running in the background, who owes what, for how long, and whether bringing it up is worth the tension.

Shared living is already an exercise in patience. Money shouldn't be the thing that makes it harder. We didn't want a complicated app with fourteen features nobody uses. We wanted something you'd actually open, actually trust, and actually use every time someone buys groceries.

That's it. No grand vision. Just the simple belief that keeping things barabar between people who live together shouldn't require this much energy.
